About Trusted Hands

Trusted Hands is a small, family-operated pet care service focused on structured, in-home visits.

Care is provided by a consistent two-person team to ensure reliability and continuity — never outsourced or passed between multiple sitters.

Your pet’s routine, comfort, and safety remain our priority at every visit.

Trusted Hands provides professional, in-home pet care focused on safety, consistency, and respect for your home.
